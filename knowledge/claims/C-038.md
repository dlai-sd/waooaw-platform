Claim ID:        C-038
Type:            LAW
Statement:       A customer has an unconditional right to pause, resume, and terminate a professional engagement or any individual Skill at any time, with billing calculated on a strict pro-rata basis from the moment of pause or termination — no minimum periods, no penalties, no forfeiture of work produced during the billing period.
Confidence:      97%
Source:          Constitution Article IX (Bill of Rights — Customer Rights: right to terminate immediately); FR-002 (trial outputs owned by customer); GENESIS Part 01 Acceptance Scenario 003 (Rahul must be able to stop the trading agent — this right must extend to all customers and all engagement types)
Depends On:      C-001 (human override absolute — the right to stop must be unconditional), C-034 (employment lifecycle — SUSPENDED and TERMINATED states exist)
Produces:        NONE
Contradicted By: NONE
Status:          RATIFIED
Reviewer Notes:  Billing is the commercial expression of constitutional rights. If a customer can constitutionally stop an agent at any moment (C-001, Emergency Stop), they must also be able to stop paying for it at that moment. Pro-rata billing is not a commercial feature — it is the financial implementation of the customer's constitutional right to pause and terminate. Any billing model that charges for periods after a customer-initiated pause is a constitutional violation, not merely a commercial dispute.
