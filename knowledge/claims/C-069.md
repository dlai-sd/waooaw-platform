Reviewer Notes:  WHY THIS IS A CONSTITUTIONAL OBLIGATION AND NOT JUST A FEATURE:

                 The distinction matters for prioritization and for future constitutional reviews.
                 A "feature" can be deprioritized. A constitutional obligation cannot be deferred
                 once the platform is live with paying customers.

                 The argument: if the platform knows (from audit_records) that DMA Skill 3 has
                 failed 4 times in 7 days and does not raise an improvement proposal, it has
                 withheld material information from Sujay (the quality steward) and continued
                 charging Dr. Mehta for a degraded service. This is indistinguishable in effect
                 from the exploitation that C-048 prohibits — the customer is not told, and the
                 billing continues.

                 SELF-IMPROVEMENT ANALYST SCOPE:
                 The Self-Improvement Analyst does NOT autonomously fix anything. It:
                 1. Detects degradation from audit_records (evidence-based, C-023 compliant)
                 2. Analyzes the failure pattern
                 3. Generates an improvement hypothesis
                 4. Raises a Skill Proposal (for Sujay's review and approval)
                 5. Notifies Sujay via Steward Assistant

                 All implementation of the improvement requires the full Tier 1/2 authorization
                 cycle (C-066). The Self-Improvement Analyst has no write access to production
                 prompt tables or agent specifications. Its only output is a GitHub Issue.

                 RELATIONSHIP TO C-049:
                 C-049 requires an agent to disclose its limitations to the customer.
                 C-069 requires the platform to disclose the agent's limitations to Sujay.
                 They are the same principle at different levels of the governance hierarchy.
