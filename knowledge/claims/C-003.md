Claim ID:        C-003
Type:            LAW
Statement:       Authority is never granted by confidence; authority is continuously licensed through constitutional evidence and may be suspended or expired when evidence warrants.
Confidence:      100%
Source:          Constitution Article III — The Second Law
Depends On:      C-002 (trust earned through evidence)
Produces:        C-010 (Proposed state as earned evidence), C-023 (PAAS model — authority is pre-licensed, not assumed)
Contradicted By: NONE
Status:          RATIFIED
Reviewer Notes:  Explicitly stated as The Second Law. "A license earned. A license that can be suspended. A license that can expire. A license that requires renewal." The architecture must implement this license model — authority expansion is a governed event, not a configuration flag. The PAAS model in Case 003 is a direct application of this law: the customer pre-licenses a Decision Space, not a capability.
