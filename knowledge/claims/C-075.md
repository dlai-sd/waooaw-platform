Statement:       WAOOAW supports a white-label reseller model where an agency (the "Reseller") deploys WAOOAW's AI agents under the reseller's own brand identity to their own clients. In white-label mode: (1) The customer-facing brand is the reseller's brand — the reseller's name, logo, colors, and domain appear everywhere the customer sees the interface. The WAOOAW name, "WoW Expert" agent names, and constitutional governance vocabulary are not visible to the reseller's clients. (2) Constitutional governance operates identically underneath — all 74 claims, all CCTs, all Evidence First obligations, all Emergency Stop guarantees apply without exception regardless of branding. White-labeling changes the presentation layer only. It cannot change, weaken, or bypass any constitutional principle. (3) The reseller takes full responsibility for their clients' experience under their brand. WAOOAW remains responsible for the constitutional quality of the underlying platform. (4) A reseller's client who exercises Emergency Stop receives the same ≤250ms guarantee as any direct WAOOAW customer. A reseller cannot disable or delay Emergency Stop for their clients.

Reviewer Notes:  WHY WHITE-LABEL DOESN'T COMPROMISE CONSTITUTION:

                 A patient who uses "Yashus AI Dental Marketing" is receiving
                 the same constitutional protections as a patient using "WoW Expert
                 Dental Marketing" directly. The Evidence First obligation, the
                 Emergency Stop guarantee, the Information Non-Exploitation pledge —
                 all apply identically. The reseller cannot configure away any
                 constitutional protection for their clients.

                 The analogy: a private label medication (same molecule, different
                 packaging) is subject to the same drug regulations as the brand-name
                 version. The regulations follow the product, not the brand.

                 WHAT WHITE-LABEL CHANGES:
                 - Customer-visible name: "WoW Expert" → reseller's agent name
                 - Portal domain: waooaw.ai → clients.yashus.in
                 - Logo + colors: WAOOAW brand → Yashus brand
                 - Email sender: noreply@waooaw.ai → noreply@yashus.in
                 - Report header: WAOOAW logo → Yashus logo

                 WHAT WHITE-LABEL NEVER CHANGES:
                 - Constitutional governance (all 74 claims apply)
                 - Evidence First enforcement
                 - Emergency Stop ≤250ms guarantee
                 - CCT pass requirements
                 - C-048/C-049 obligations
                 - DPDPA compliance
                 - Data isolation between clients
