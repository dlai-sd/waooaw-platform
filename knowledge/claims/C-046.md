Claim ID:        C-046
Type:            LAW
Statement:       The WAOOAW platform must operate under the same constitutional governance framework that governs its customer-facing agents. Platform operations — monitoring, incident response, billing reconciliation, agent health assessment, and constitutional compliance auditing — must themselves be performed by constitutional agents with defined Decision Spaces, evidence-recorded actions, and human override rights. An unmonitored, ungoverned platform operations layer is a constitutional violation because it creates a class of actions that affect customers but are not subject to the platform's own governance framework.
Confidence:      95%
Source:          C-001 (human override unconditional — this right applies to platform operations affecting a customer, not just skill execution); C-002 (trust earned through evidence — platform operations that are invisible to customers cannot earn trust); GENESIS Part 01 — the institution's credibility depends on its governance applying consistently to all actions, not selectively to customer-facing ones; Constitution Article IX — customer rights are violated if platform operations can affect their engagements without oversight.
Depends On:      C-001 (human override), C-002 (trust through evidence), C-003 (authority licensing)
Produces:        Domain 12 capabilities (Platform Operations), Platform Operations Agent spec, DP-017 (Autonomous Platform Operations)
Contradicted By: NONE
Status:          RATIFIED
Reviewer Notes:  The most dangerous class of constitutional violations is the one that happens in the dark. If the platform's billing agent double-charges a customer, the customer has no visibility. If the platform's monitoring agent incorrectly terminates an employment contract, the customer has no evidence chain. If the platform has no operations agent at all, then human engineers perform operational tasks with no evidence records, no constitutional constraints, and no audit trail. This claim establishes that the platform must eat its own cooking: every platform operation that can affect a customer must be performed by a constitutionally governed agent, with evidence records, customer notification rights, and override rights. The Platform Operations Agent is the institutional expression of this claim.
