Claim ID:        C-053
Type:            LAW
Statement:       Every Digital Professional agent that operates in a domain where external signals (environmental, market, operational) can materially affect a customer's outcomes MUST maintain a continuous Signal Intelligence Layer — a parallel, always-on observation capability that monitors defined signal feeds, evaluates signal materiality against each customer's current state, and proactively communicates actionable insights before the customer asks. Proactive intelligence is not a feature — it is a constitutional obligation. An agent that waits for a farmer to ask "will it hail?" before checking the weather forecast has failed its professional duty. A sentinel professional senses risk and communicates; a passive tool waits to be queried. The materiality threshold, urgency classification, and communication timing rules must be explicitly declared in the agent specification and enforced at runtime. No proactive communication may occur without a constitutional evidence record. Emergency alerts (materiality ≥ 0.90, URGENCY_CLASS = CRITICAL) are budget-exempt — the customer's UsageUnit balance cannot block a constitutional obligation to warn.

Reviewer Notes:  This claim fills the most important gap in the current agent model: agents are instruction-takers by default. C-053 draws the constitutional line — certain professional types have a duty of care that exceeds reactive assistance. The weather alert analogy is exact: a tornado warning system is not an AI assistant that answers weather queries. It is a professional obligation infrastructure that operates continuously and communicates proactively when risk crosses a threshold. Three implementation safeguards are mandatory: (1) TRAI compliance — proactive messages outside the 24-hour service window require HSM pre-approved templates; CRITICAL alerts are governed by emergency exemptions. (2) Budget exemption — URGENCY_CLASS=CRITICAL signals are always emergency_exempt:true, regardless of UsageUnit balance; budget cannot block professional duty. (3) Evidence trail — every proactive communication produces a CAL evidence record with the signal source, materiality score, and customer state at the time of alert; this enables PMFBY insurance claims (agricultural), audit of professional diligence (all agents), and constitutional compliance verification. The gap this closes: DMA agent currently has no signal watching. Trading agent has no pre-session alert model. Agricultural agent has the intent but no formal architectural standard. C-053 standardises this across all applicable agents.
