Reviewer Notes:  The 90% uniform floor replaces the prior tiered system (CE=90%, BP/PR=85%, AIR=80%, Web=75%).
                 The uniform floor reflects two insights:
                 1. Differential floors create pressure to classify work as "lower tier" to escape the higher standard.
                    A single universal floor removes this incentive.
                 2. WAOOAW customers (Suresh, Dr. Mehta, Rahul) trust autonomous agents with their businesses.
                    A bug in the Business Platform is not a lower-stakes bug than a bug in the CE.
                    Every service receives the same constitutional quality floor.

                 Mutation testing targets remain tiered (CE ≥75%, others ≥70%) because mutation
                 testing measures test quality, not quantity — and CE is the highest-stakes service.
