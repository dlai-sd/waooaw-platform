Claim ID:        C-031
Type:            LAW
Statement:       No architectural or technology decision of significance may be implemented without a corresponding Architecture Decision Record (ADR) that documents the decision, alternatives rejected, trade-offs, and constitutional basis.
Confidence:      100%
Source:          GENESIS Part 02 — Decision Classification: "Class 2 — Frozen: Architectural decisions frozen by the Chief Engineer. Cannot be changed without an Architecture Decision Record (ADR)." and "Never implement an undocumented Class 2 decision."
Depends On:      C-008 (Constitutional Chain — GENESIS is above architecture in the chain)
Produces:        NONE (meta-law governing how architecture is recorded)
Contradicted By: NONE
Status:          RATIFIED
Reviewer Notes:  Produced in response to R-001 Gap 1. This claim is the constitutional authorization for all 15 ADRs in the adr/ folder. Without this claim, ADRs exist as a convention rather than as a constitutional requirement. The EA needs this to have formal authority to require ADRs from all offices. "Significance" is defined by GENESIS Class 2 (technology stack, database selection, deployment model) — not every minor decision, but all decisions that cannot be reversed without an ADR process.
