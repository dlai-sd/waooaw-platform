Claim ID:        C-007
Type:            LAW
Statement:       No evidence in any ledger may be retroactively modified or deleted; all ledger operations are append-only by constitutional mandate.
Confidence:      100%
Source:          Constitution Article XI (Constitutional Floors): "No evidence in any ledger may be retroactively modified or deleted."
Depends On:      C-005 (Three-Ledger Model establishes the ledgers)
Produces:        C-027 (Constitutional Audit Ledger must be append-only at the database layer)
Contradicted By: NONE
Status:          RATIFIED
Reviewer Notes:  Stated as a Constitutional Floor — meaning it cannot be disabled by configuration, commercial agreement, or platform update. The architectural consequence is absolute: the database schema must enforce append-only behavior, not merely rely on application-level enforcement. A migration that includes UPDATE or DELETE on ledger tables is a Constitutional Floor violation, not merely a data architecture error. ADR-011 (Database Migration Strategy) ratified this with the "no destructive migrations on constitutional schema" rule.
