## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. WAOOAW uses cookies and similar technologies (local storage, session storage) to operate the platform and, with your consent, to improve it.

## 4. Your Choices

**Analytics cookies:** On your first visit, a consent banner will appear. You may accept or decline analytics cookies. You can change your preference at any time in **Settings → Cookie Preferences**.

**Necessary and functional cookies** cannot be declined as they are required for the platform to function. Declining them would prevent login and use of the platform.

**Do Not Track (DNT):** If your browser sends a DNT signal, analytics cookies will not be set regardless of your consent preference.

---

## 5. How to Manage Cookies

You may also control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy
- **Firefox:** Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Clearing cookies will log you out of the Platform. Your language and theme preferences will reset.
