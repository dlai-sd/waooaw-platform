## 2. What You Can Raise a Grievance About

You may raise a formal grievance regarding any of the following:

1. **Personal data** — how your personal data is collected, stored, used, shared, or deleted
2. **WoW Expert behaviour** — any action taken by your WoW Expert that you believe was outside its authorised Decision Space or violated the constitutional framework
3. **Constitutional rights** — any situation where you believe your constitutional rights (as set out in Article IX of the WAOOAW Constitution and the Terms of Service) have not been respected
4. **Content** — content generated by a WoW Expert that you believe violates applicable law or WAOOAW's content standards
5. **Billing** — subscription charges, refunds, or billing accuracy disputes
6. **Account access** — unauthorised access to your account or data

## 3. How to Raise a Grievance

### Step 1 — Customer Support (first resolution)
Most issues are resolved quickly by the support team.
- Email: customersupport@dlaisd.com
- WhatsApp: +91 8888912344
- Response target: **2 working days**

### Step 2 — Formal Grievance to Grievance Officer
If the support team has not resolved your issue within 5 working days, or if the nature of the grievance requires formal handling:
- Email: yogesh.khandge@dlaisd.com
- Subject line: "Formal Grievance — [brief description]"
- Include: your registered contact details, a description of the grievance, and any relevant reference numbers
- **Response obligation: within 30 days of receipt** (IT Rules 2021 — Rule 3(1)(c))

### Step 3 — Escalation
If the Grievance Officer's response does not resolve your grievance:

**For personal data grievances:**
- Approach the **Data Protection Board of India** (established under DPDPA 2023)

**For consumer grievances:**
- National Consumer Helpline: **1800-11-4000**
- E-Daakhil portal: edaakhil.nic.in
- Appropriate Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission

**For content grievances (IT Rules 2021):**
- If your grievance relates to content that has not been actioned within 15 days of a formal complaint under Rule 3(1)(c), you may escalate to the appellate mechanism designated under the IT Rules.

## 4. Constitutional Grievance — Special Process

If your grievance is that a WoW Expert acted **outside its constitutional Decision Space** or that a **Constitutional Floor was breached** (e.g., Emergency Stop was not honoured within 250ms; audit records were modified; data was accessed across tenant boundaries):

1. File a formal grievance per Step 2 above, marking it "Constitutional Grievance"
2. The Grievance Officer will treat this as a **Constitutional Blocker** — the highest priority internal classification
3. Investigation will begin within **24 hours** of receipt
4. You will receive a preliminary response within **5 working days** and a full investigation report within **21 days**
5. If a constitutional violation is confirmed, DLAISD is obligated to: correct the violation, update the audit record, notify you of the outcome, and implement preventive measures

---

## 5. Acknowledgement and Records

Every grievance received will be acknowledged within **24 hours** with a reference number. This reference number must be used in all subsequent communications.

DLAISD maintains a register of all grievances received, their nature, and their resolution status. This register is available to statutory authorities on request.

---

## 6. No Retaliation

DLAISD will not suspend, restrict, or penalise any customer for raising a genuine grievance in good faith, regardless of the outcome of the grievance.
